<h1>Examples of the echo directive</h1>
Filename: <b><!--#echo var="DOCUMENT_NAME"--></b>
<br>
URI: <b><!--#echo var="DOCUMENT_URI"--></b>
<br>
Local time: <b><!--#echo var="DATE_LOCAL"--></b>
<br>
GMT: <b><!--#echo var="DATE_GMT"--></b>
<br>
Last modified: <b><!--#echo var="LAST_MODIFIED"--></b>
